You Can Make An Investment in The Orlando Magic Wheels and Keep The Team Spinning To Victory.

Members of the Orlando Magic Wheels (individually & as a team) participate in communnity Service Exhibitions to promote disability awareness in the Central Florida area. Educating “Temporarily Enabled” people of all ages is a cornerstone of our Organization. Teaching physically disabled persons to become better athletes and team players is an obvious fundamental of our core value. However, without assistance and funding from the community we live in, the burden falls to our players and their families to pay for the things that keep us going.
